Why Surfing?

I was born in the Bay Area, and when I was little, my mom would take me to watch surfing competitions along the coast. Even before I could fully understand it, I was drawn to the ocean, the energy, and the lifestyle.

When I was really young and would cry, my mom would play Jack Johnson to calm me down. He’s not only a songwriter, but also an incredible surfer from Hawaii — and someone who shares the same faith as me. That connection made surfing feel even more personal growing up.

Ever since I was little, I’ve wanted to live by the beach and surf every day. It’s always been more than just something fun — it’s something I’ve always imagined as part of my life.
